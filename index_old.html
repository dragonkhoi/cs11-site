CS11SI: How to Make VR: Introduction to Virtual Reality Design and Development

Stanford / Computer Science / Fall 2018

Course Description

  • In this hands-on, experiential course, students will design and develop virtual reality applications. You'll learn how to use the Unity game engine, the most popular platform for creating immersive applications. The class will teach the design best-practices and the creation pipeline for VR applications, and will include tangents that explore sister fields such as augmented reality and 360 video. Students will work in groups to present a final project in building an application for the Oculus Go headset. Enrollment is limited and by rolling application only. Whether or not you are enrolled in Axess you must complete an application. In other words, enrolling in Axess does not guarantee enrollment. Applications will be accepted through September 25th, 2018. Prerequisite: CS 106A or equivalent.
